Entrance Examinations

Techno Main Salt Lake offers admission to its prestigious undergraduate and postgraduate programs through various national and state-level entrance examinations. These exams assess candidates' aptitude, technical knowledge, and readiness for professional courses in engineering, management, and computer applications.

The WBJEE (West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination) is the primary gateway for admission to undergraduate engineering programs at Techno Main Salt Lake. It tests candidates in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, ensuring a strong foundation for engineering studies.

JEE Main, conducted by the National Testing Agency, is another pathway for B.Tech admissions. It is a highly competitive exam that evaluates students' proficiency in science and mathematics, offering opportunities to join top-tier institutions like ours.

For lateral entry into the second year of B.Tech programs, JELET (Joint Entrance for Lateral Entry) is required. This exam is designed for diploma holders seeking to advance their engineering education.

Postgraduate aspirants can pursue M.Tech programs through GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering), which tests comprehensive technical knowledge and is widely recognized for its rigor.

For MBA admissions, the CAT (Common Admission Test) is accepted, evaluating candidates' quantitative, verbal, and analytical skills, preparing them for leadership roles in business.

JECA (Joint Entrance for Computer Applications) is the key to our MCA program, focusing on mathematical and computer science aptitude, ideal for students aiming for careers in IT.
