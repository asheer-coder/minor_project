Eligibility Criteria for Entrance Examinations

Admission Requirements

WBJEE (West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination)

  • Candidates must have passed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry/Biology/Computer Science as compulsory subjects.
  • Minimum 45% marks in the above subjects (40% for reserved categories).
  • Must be a resident of West Bengal or meet domicile requirements.

JEE Main

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics, and one of Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Technical Vocational subject.
  • Minimum 75% marks in 10+2 (65% for SC/ST) or be in the top 20 percentile of the respective board.
  • No age limit, but candidates must have passed 10+2 in the last 2-3 years.

JELET (Joint Entrance for Lateral Entry)

  • Diploma in Engineering/Technology with at least 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).
  • B.Sc. degree holders with Mathematics at 10+2 level are also eligible.
  • No upper age limit for diploma holders; B.Sc. candidates must be under 27 years.

GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering)

  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering/Technology (4 years after 10+2 or 3 years after Diploma).
  • Final-year students of qualifying degrees are eligible.
  • No minimum percentage or age limit.

CAT (Common Admission Test)

  • Bachelor’s degree with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/PwD).
  • Final-year students of bachelor’s programs are eligible.
  • No age limit.

JECA (Joint Entrance for Computer Applications)

  • Bachelor’s degree in any discipline with Mathematics at 10+2 or graduation level.
  • Minimum 60% marks in 10+2 and graduation (55% for reserved categories).
  • Candidates must be Indian nationals and meet West Bengal domicile requirements.
